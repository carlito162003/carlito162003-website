About the Project

“Catnip.” is an RPG Maker Game being developed as of right now.
This project is relatively new and barely any progress has been
made. There is no release date right now. Any progress or updates
will be uploaded on this site and my socials. The game has overly
sensitive topics. Viewer Discretion is Advised.

What is CatNip?

Investigate around the world that revolves around “It.” With “It.”
having no clue or idea of what they are or what they are doing it
is your job to put the pieces together and unlock the story and
origin of what lies behind “it.”
